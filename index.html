CryptoPriceTrends

CryptoPriceTrends is an Android application that provides real-time cryptocurrency price alerts with voice notifications.

CryptoPriceTrends does not facilitate cryptocurrency trading, buying, or selling. It provides price tracking and alerts only.

Features

  • Real-time cryptocurrency price tracking (no trading or transactions)
  • Popup and voice alerts
  • Background alerts (PRO+)

Pricing

  • FREE – Limited voice alerts
  • PRO – Extended voice alerts
  • PRO+ – Unlimited voice alerts & background alerts
