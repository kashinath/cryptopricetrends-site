CryptoPriceTrends

CryptoPriceTrends is an Android application that provides real-time cryptocurrency market price tracking and alert notifications.

This application is for informational purposes only. It does not support cryptocurrency trading, buying, selling, investment services, or financial advice.

Features

  • Real-time crypto price monitoring
  • Popup and voice-based price alerts
  • Background alerts for PRO+ users

Pricing

  • FREE – Limited voice alerts
  • PRO – Extended voice alerts
  • PRO+ – Unlimited voice alerts and background alerts
